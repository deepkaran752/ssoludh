HOW WE MAKE A DIFFERENCE?


S.S.O. is a NON-GOVERNMENTAL ORGANIZATION (NGO) which is led by the youth of our nation to bring change in the society by eradicating the evil practices of child labor and child begging by proving them education through proper schooling and skill development classes. Further, working and taking action on some of the significant environmental issues (or concerns) which are kept unseen (or being ignored) by the administration (or by the government) As we live in the world's largest democracy, we here proudly practice social & political activism by raising our voice against any unjust act by any Public servant or Govt authority.

OUR STORY


S.S.O. was established as a NON-GOVERNMENTAL (NGO) & NOT FOR PROFIT ORGANIZATION (NFPO) by the group of school students on 29 July 2019 and was primarily working on environmental issues but later on observing some more prevalent issues ongoing in the society like forced child labor and practice of child begging, the organization took initiative and decided to work on it with the utmost priority and started A pilot project in the city of LUDHIANA by surveying and collecting data on educational status in various slum cluster areas of the city which later turned into a structuralized project - UNNATI. After the successful work of 2 years, In the year 2021 Organization was officially registered under Societies Registration Act, 1860 and subsequently there was no way back, to eradicate hunger among poverty-stricken students allocation of raw food & ration was being started under PROJECT ANNAPURNA, For taking action on some serious environmental issues and raising awareness on climate change among citizens..PROJECT SWACHHTA was launched. Along sides, mainstream projects several small scale events and drives are also regularly conducted by the team for eg BLOOD DONATION CAMPS & VACCINATION CAMPS are being organized on a regular basis.
